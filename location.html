Nekemte city




Location

Nekemte town is located in Oromia National Regional State, in East Wollega Zone, Nekemte Zuria Woreda, at a Distance of 331 Km from Addis Ababa. Its astronomical location is 9º 04’ North Latitude and 36º 30’ East Longitude.
